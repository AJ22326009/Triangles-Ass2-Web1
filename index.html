TRIANGLE AND ITS TYPES

INTRODUCTION

“Tri” means 3 and angle means the amount of turn made by a point.Therefore a triangle is a plane shape with three sides and angles. The angles of a triangle sum up to 180°.

TYPES OF TRIANGLES

The following are the types of triangles:

  • Equilateral triangle
  • Isosceles triangle
  • Scalene triangle
  • Right angle triangle

EQUILATERAL TRIANGLE

Properties

  1. All sides are equal.
  2. All angles are equal.
  3. Each angle is equal to 60°.

ISOSCELES TRIANGLE

Properties

  1. Two sides are equal.
  2. Two angles are equal.

SCALENE TRIANGLE

Properties

  1. None of the sides are equal.
  2. None of the angles are equal.

RIGHT-ANGLED TRIANGLE

Properties

  1. One of its angles is 90°.
